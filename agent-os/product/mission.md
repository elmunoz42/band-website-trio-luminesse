# Mission

## Product

Trio Luminesse needs a warm, polished public website that introduces the trio, communicates the feel of the music, and turns interest from event hosts into booking conversations.

## Audience

- Private event hosts in Santa Barbara and the Central Coast
- Wedding planners and couples
- Community arts organizers, venues, and local cultural programs
- Friends, fans, and listeners looking for upcoming availability

## Core Value

The site should make Trio Luminesse feel personal, soulful, and easy to book. It should express the blend of Brazilian rhythm, jazz-pop movement, soul warmth, and intimate singer-songwriter storytelling without feeling like a generic musician template.

## Product Principles

- Put the band and booking path in the first viewport.
- Keep copy direct, human, and performance-oriented.
- Treat WordPress as the editable content source and Next.js as the presentation layer.
- Make the frontend easy to deploy on Vercel once the WordPress backend is ready on SiteGround.
